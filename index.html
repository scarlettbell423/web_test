FUNDACIÓN CON CORA

Fundación Con Cora de la artista KAROL G

Fundación Con Cora was a foundation founded by Colombian superstar Karol G for women put in vulnerable situations. Whether it's women in prisons, homeless, or single mothers barely making it in society, Con Cora's main goal is to help women grow and thrive in the world.

    How to help

    To support the Con Cora Foundation, you can make a donation of at least $1 USD. They have networks of people to work with who can help you contribute to the multiple program like hgiher education, training, support for sports, and much much more to help women of all ages succeed.

About

All about KAROL G

Carolina Giraldo Navarro was born in Medellín, Colombia on February 14, 1991. Her father, Juan Guillermo Giraldo was also a musician, and she says that he was her biggest supporter in music all throughout her life. Being a woman in a latin genre mainly pursued by men (reggaeton), she faced much criticism and was told she would never make it in the industry. After being sexually preyed ob by a former manager at 16 years old, she quit music and moved to New York to escape. However, she picked it up again as she felt that she could not leave music completely. Now, she is an international super star recognized all over the world for her one of a kind talent.

Awards and Achievements

Throughout Karol G's music career, she was one 37 awards and has been nominated for 93. She's been nominated for two Grammy awards, 5 People's Choice Awards, 10 MTV music awards, 18 Latin Grammy Awards, 42 Billboard Latin Music Awards, 7 American Music Awards, and much much more.
