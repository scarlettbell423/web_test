FUNDACIÓN CON CORA

FUNDACIÓN CON CORA de la artista Karol G

FUNDACIÓN CON CORA was a foundation created by Karol G with its main goal being to help vulnerable women everywhere. With the foundation's help, women who are in prison, homeless, hospitalized, single mothers, and women who live in dangerous communities are all getting supported by Karol G's team, led by her older sister Jessica.

    HOW TO HELP

    One of the many ways to help Con Cora is by donating atleast $1 USD (or whatever currency equivlent). Another way to help is to find volunteering opportunities, like submitting projects for collaboration. You can also attend charity events such as "Con Cora Land" to help support.

ALBUMS AND ACCOMPLISHMENTS

Karol G's history making projects and tours

Karol G is one of the most influential artists in Latin Music of all time. She made her break through with her debut album "UNSTOPPABLE", and her single "Ahora Me Llama" featuring bad bunny. She gained more and more popularity with her later albums, like "Ocean", "KG0516", "MAÑANA SERÁ BONITO", and "MAÑANA SERÁ BONITO (BICHOTA SEASON)". All of these albums have legendary features including Shakira, Nicki Minaj, Ozuna, Maluma, Romeo Santos, and much much more. She was the first Latina artist in history to do an all sold-out stadium tour across the world with 65 shows, selling 2 million tickets in over 16 countries- grossing over 155 million dollars.

All about Karol G

Carolina Giraldo Navarro was born on February 14, 1991 in Medellín, Colombia and is the youngest of 3 sisters. Ever since she was little, she was always singing for family and friends, and her father was always her biggest encouragement for music having been an aspiring musician himself. Karol throughout her adolescence and early adult hood performed travelling from town to town in Colombia, and she veen performed on a Colombian spinoff version of the X-factor, being eliminated early on. When she was 16 years old, she moved to Bogotá, Colombia's capital alone. Her then manager sexually preyed on her in his home. Being so disillusioned by this, she moved to New York with her aunt to study English and business and quit music. However, after many month, she picked music up again and is now known as the international sensation we see her as today.
